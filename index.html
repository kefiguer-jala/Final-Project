FINAL PROJECT

Software

The software sector of CIT focuses on the design, development, deployment, and maintenance of software applications and systmes. The CIT offers its Computer and Information Technology degree for the software sector.

Networking

The networking sector of the CIT deals with the design, implementation, and management of computer networks that enable communication between devices. The CIT offers the Computing Infrastructure and Network Engineering Technology major as part of its degree program.

Data Management

The data management side of CIT involves collecting, storing, organizing, and securing data so it can be easily accessed, analyzed, and used. The CIT degree program offers its Data Analytics, Technologies and Applications major for the data management field.

Cybersecurity

The cybersecurity field of information technology on protecting digital systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. The CIT offers its Cybersecurity major as part of its degree plan for the cybersecurity field.

APPLIED COMPUTING

A software developer creates computer programs and applications that help users perform tasks or solve problems. Major suggested: Computer Information Technology.

A security analyst protects computer systems and networks from cyber threats like hackers, viruses, or data breaches. Major suggested: Cybersecurity.

A data analyst focuses on organizing, managing, and maintaining data stored in databases. Major suggested: Data Analytics, Technologies and Applications.

A network architect designs and plans the structure of an organization's computer network. Major suggested: Computing Infrastructure and Network Engineering Technology.
